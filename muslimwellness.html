Questions

Frequently Asked

Where is this place located?

990 Gainsborough Rd, London, ON N6H 5L4

Do you offer Friday prayer services?

Yes we offer 1 Friday prayer services weekly at 2:30 PM or 1:30 PM depending on the time of the year with Daylight Savings Time.

What is different about this organization?

We're aim to provide a traditional yet different experience through serving, developing and empowering.

Are all 5 daily prayers offered?

Yes all daily prayers will be offered. See prayer times for details.

How can I get involved?

There are many opportunities to get involved with the Muslim Wellness Connection as well as other Centres and Mosques around the city. Get in touch with us using the Contact Us page.

Have any other questions?

Get in touch with us and we'll be glad to help assist in finding the right answers to your questions.
